Spicy Peanut Soup with Sweet Potato + Kale

INGREDIENTS

  • 8 ounces DeLallo spirali pasta (okay, you don’t have to use spirali, but it is SO FUN)

  • half an onion, diced

  • 1 jalapeño, minced

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1/4 cup DeLallo sun-dried tomatoes packed in oil, sliced or chopped

  • one 14-ounce can fire roasted tomatoes

  • one 14-ounce can light coconut milk

  • 2 cups water

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • up to 1/2 cup reserved pasta water salt and pepper to taste

  • 1/2 cup chopped peanuts

  • 1/4 cup peanut butter

  • 1/2 cups kale, stems removed, chopped

    • a big bunch of chopped chives or parsley parmesan for serving

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Cook the pasta: Cook the pasta according to package directions.
  2. Sauté the good stuff: Heat a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Use a little bit of the oil from the sun-dried tomatoes to sauté the garlic and sun-dried tomatoes until soft and fragrant, 2-3 minutes.

  4. Make the sauce: Add the wine; let it sizzle out and reduce so the flavor gets real good.

  5. Add the cream and spinach; bring to a simmer. Season with salt and pepper.

  6. Finish: Stir in the cooked pasta, adding the reserved pasta water as needed to help coat the noodles in the sauce.

    7. Finish with chives and Parmesan and a top-up on your glass of wine. SO GOOD..
  7. Add sweet potatoes. I like to brown them a little bit with the aromatics to get them nice and flavorful.

  8. Add tomatoes, coconut milk, water, spices, and peanuts. Simmer until sweet potatoes are fork-tender.

  9. Add peanut butter and kale. Simmer until everything is thick, creamy, and delicious. Top with more peanuts and a little cilantro if you’re obsessed like me.
